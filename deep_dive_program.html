You're Not Doing This Alone

Coaching Support

This program is a partnership. Schramm (DLC) and the Instructional Coach are embedded throughout the year so every teacher grows with confidence.

🤝

August PD Launch

Start of Year · Schramm (DLC)

Every teacher meets 1:1 with Schramm during August PD to choose their focus area and understand the program. Teachers then set their T-TESS goal with their appraiser.

📅

Ongoing Check-Ins

Throughout the Week · Schramm (DLC) & Anuwe (IC)

The IC meets with teams throughout the week. Schramm joins Monday planning meetings and is available Tuesdays by appointment.

💬

Individual Support

As Needed · Schramm (DLC) & Anuwe (IC)

Targeted 1:1 coaching from Schramm and Anuwe as teachers work through specific strategies. Self-curated strategies approved through a brief conversation.

Grounded In

Framework Connections

T-TESS

The badge portfolio is ready-made evidence for technology integration and instructional goal documentation. The rubric maps directly to appraiser evaluation language.

Future Ready Framework

Every reflection asks how the work moved the classroom toward being Future Ready — connecting pedagogy directly to the district's vision for students.

Understanding by Design

Evidence requirements work backwards from the goal: class impact and deep student thinking — not strategy completion or badge count.
