Submit a Badge

You Did the Work. Let's Celebrate It.

When you have completed a strategy — reflection written and artifact ready — submit your badge below. You can submit badges throughout the year as you complete them.

Ready to Submit?

Before you click submit, make sure you have everything ready using the checklist on the right. Each badge submission should include your reflection, your artifact, and evidence of what changed for your students.

You can submit badges throughout the year — you don't have to wait until the end. Submitting as you go makes the process easier and gives you a chance to get feedback along the way from Schramm or Anuwe.
